Food licence or FSSAI licence is granted to all manufacturers, traders, restaurants carrying out any food business which may involve manufacturing or processing.

The persons involved in the food business, such as manufacturing, transportation, distribution and storage of food articles/products are also mandatorily required to have a FSSAI registration to carry on their food business.

A food licence is essential to be able to be a legal producer and distributor of food. It is a requirement to pass hygiene and safety regulations to provide clean food to the masses.
