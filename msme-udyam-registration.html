Frequently Asked Questions - UDYAM Registration

What is UDYAM? MSME?

General certificate that allows micro, small, and medium enterprises to register themselves with the government.

Why is UDYAM registration required?

This certification is essential if you seek legal and operational certification for small or medium businesses. The Ministry of MSME, Government of India, conducts Udyam registration for micro and small businesses.

Who needs UDYAM registration certificate?

MSME entrepreneurs to obtain the benefits provided to MSMEs.

How does UDYAM benefit MSME?

Provides the MSME with a commendation certificate and a unique identity number.

A proprietorship, partnership, firm, with an investment below Rs.50 crore and annual turnover below Rs.250 crore are eligible.
