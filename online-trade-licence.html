What is a trade licence?

A trade license is a legal document that permits an individual or a business to engage in a specific trade, business, or commercial activity within a particular geographic area. It is issued by the local municipal corporation or the concerned authority, depending on the regulations of the region.

Why is there a trade licence?

A trade license is mandatory for businesses to comply with regulations, operate legally, generate revenue, and ensure safety standards. It helps prevent tax evasion and illegal practices and identifies unregistered businesses.
